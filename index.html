BilBareed

BilBareed Story

BilBareed is a leader in the movement to help the world deliver E-Cards, which includes both Greetings and invitations.

We believe there are at least half a billion people who can benefit from E-Cards, probably more. To join our movement, sign up for an account at bilbareed website now.

Background

BilBareed is an outgrowth of the traditional greeting cards and invitations. in the current era where everything is becoming digitally organized, we can't see why greeting and invitations should be any different. the ability to deliver your feelings and emotions through E-Cards customized for different culteures and occasions is our speciality.

THE FOUNDERS

BilBareed was founded in 2016 by Basma Al-Bakree, Dana Farouki, and Huda Al-Lawati.
